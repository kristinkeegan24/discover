Hotels

Around every corner of Dublin awaits a new and exciting experience with various types of cuisine ranging from Irish to Italian, and of course a Chinese chipper-style shop selling Ireland's famous 'Spice Bag'.

Images with Titles Descriptions

The Marker Hotel

Anantara The Marker Dublin Hotel is a sleek, contemporary landmark inspired by the elements and overlooking the waterfront of Grand Canal Square.

Riu Plaza The Gresham

The new Hotel Riu Plaza The Gresham Dublin is located in the heart of Dublin, in a historic building on the famous O’Connell Street, and become one of the city's emblematic sites.

Royal Marine Hotel

The historic 4-star Royal Marine Hotel in Dún Laoghaire features 228 luxury bedrooms that have been updated with both contemporary and traditional furnishings.

Powerscourt Hotel

Powerscourt Hotel, a Palladian style palace which brings together natural beauty and dignified accommodations to offer its guests an unforgettable retreat.
